Deaths directly related to tobacco (ICD-10 – F17) or alcohol (ICD-10 – F10) have been excluded from the analysis. Causes of death data undergo a revision process. Data for 2018 and 2019 are preliminary (indicated by 'x' in most of the plots) and subject to another two rounds of revision. Data for 2017 are revised (indicated by 'o' in most of the plots) and subject to another round of revision. Data for 2016 and earlier years are final. Data with numbers of deaths between 1 and 5 have been removed and this can be visible as gaps in the data series. Zero values have not been affected by confidentialisation.

NOTE: Data for intents Intentional, Undetermined and Other are only available for ages 15 to 64 and All ages within each state/territory due to small numbers. Data for Other as intent in Australia are only available for ages 15 to 64 and All ages due to small numbers.

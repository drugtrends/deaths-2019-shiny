Deaths directly related to tobacco (ICD-10 – F17) or alcohol (ICD-10 – F10) have been excluded from the analysis. Causes of death data for 2017, 2018 and 2019 are not final and subject to further revisions process; data for 2016 and earlier years are final. Small numbers of death have been randomly assigned to protect the confidentiality of individuals. Zero values have not been affected by confidentialisation.

NOTE:Data for intents Intentional, Undetermined and Other are only available for ages 15 to 64 and All ages within each state/territory due to small numbers. Data for Other as intent in Australia are only available for ages 15 to 64 and All ages due to small numbers.

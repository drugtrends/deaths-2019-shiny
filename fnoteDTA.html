Deaths directly attributable to tobacco (ICD-10 – F17) or alcohol (ICD-10 – F10) have been excluded from the analysis. Results presented here include alcohol only as a substance present in drug-induced deaths. Causes of death data undergo a revision process. Data for 2018 and 2019 are preliminary (indicated by 'x' in most of the plots) and subject to another two rounds of revision. Data for 2017 are revised (indicated by 'o' in most of the plots) and subject to another round of revision. Data for 2016 and earlier years are final. Data with number of deaths between 1 and 5 have been removed and this can be visible as gaps in the data series. Zero values have not been affected by confidentialisation.

Presented here are drug-induced deaths where a substance from a broader drug class (in CAPITAL LETTERS) or particular drug type (in lowercase letters) was present in drug-induced death. Note, multiple drugs may be involved in one death. Refer to Notes tab for examples of drugs commonly assigned to each category.

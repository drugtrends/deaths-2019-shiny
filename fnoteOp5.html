These data refer to deaths where opioid toxicity was considered to be the underlying cause of death. Causes of death data undergo a revision process. Data for 2018 and 2019 are preliminary (indicated by 'x' in most of the plots) and subject to another two rounds of revision. Data for 2017 are revised (indicated by 'o' in most of the plots) and subject to another round of revision. Data for 2016 and earlier years are final. Data with number of deaths less than or equal to 5 have been removed and this can be visible as gaps in the data series.

Natural and semi-synthetic opioids include morphine, oxycodone and codeine (ICD-10 Code T40.2). Synthetic opioids include fentanyl, tramadol and pethidine (ICD-10 Code T40.4). Other and unspecified opioids comprise opioid-induced deaths that are not attributed to specific opioids (ICD-10 Code T40.6).

Data on Other and unspecified opioids are not available for Intentional intent and Undetermined intent due to small numbers.

Plots also showing "All drug-induced deaths" display data on drug-induced deaths involving the corresponding opioid type. For your information, these data on drug-induced deaths are also shown under the plots for "Drug-induced deaths by drug, jurisdiction and sex".

These data refer to deaths where cocaine toxicity was considered to be the underlying cause of death. Causes of death data undergo a revision process. Data for 2018 and 2019 are preliminary (indicated by 'x' in most of the plots) and subject to another two rounds of revision. Data for 2017 are revised (indicated by 'o' in most of the plots) and subject to another round of revision. Data for 2016 and earlier years are final. Data with number of deaths between 1 and 5 have been removed and this can be visible as gaps in the data series. Zero values have not been affected by confidentialisation.

NOTE: Results by sex unavailable due to small numbers.

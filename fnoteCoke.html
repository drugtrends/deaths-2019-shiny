These data refer to deaths where cocaine toxicity was considered to be the underlying cause of death. Causes of death data for 2017, 2018 and 2019 are not final and subject to further revisions process; data for 2016 and earlier years are final. Small numbers of death have been randomly assigned to protect the confidentiality of individuals. Zero values have not been affected by confidentialisation.

NOTE:Results by sex unavailable due to small numbers.
